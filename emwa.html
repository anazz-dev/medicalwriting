Exploring "Biotechnology, AI, and Trust"

Published on: June 01, 2022

Introduction

The rapidly evolving fields of biotechnology and artificial intelligence (AI) present unprecedented opportunities and challenges. Biotechnology, involving the application of living organisms in creating or modifying products, intersects powerfully with AI, a disruptive tool that's accelerating innovation and revolutionizing data processing capabilities in this domain.

Data and Regulations in Biotechnology and AI

However, along with these advancements comes a daunting task: handling immense volumes of data and the intricacies of AI regulations in biotech applications. Establishing trust and understanding becomes paramount as we navigate through this intricate landscape.
